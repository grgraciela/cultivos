Organic Crops

Hello there, we welcome you to our webpage to soak up new knowledge about organic crops, orchards and how to take care of them.


Organic

What is organic crops?

Organic crops are formed using organic elements without the intervention of any chemical substance or artificial fertilizer. Therefore, it is recommended to use natural alternatives such as orange peel, eggs, etc.


How to prevent pest intervention?

Some basic actions to prevent pests are: a clean environment is needed, that is, one that is not contaminated with dirty elements; opt for a variety of crops, take good care of the plantation, the structure and nutrients of the soil, adjust irrigation well, etc. Some examples of natural pesticides are:

  • Eggshell.
  • Water with garlic.
  • Cow milk.
  • Nettle and tomato.

Have you ever thought about having an organic garden?

Here are some benefits of having an organic garden

  • 1. SAVES MONEY Growing your own vegetables can save you a lot of money! By having a vegetable garden in your backyard there is no need for you to spend money on gas to drive to the store or to purchase lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, etc. Instead, if you feel like having carrots for dinner, you can go in your backyard and harvest some of your own carrots!

  • 2. HEALTHIER Another plus to having an organic garden is that your herbs, fruits, and vegetables are not treated with harmful chemicals! When foods are organic, they reduce the risk of spreading diseases to consumers. Healthier food is also known to increase mental and physical health!

  • 3. MORE FLAVOUR Organic foods have higher vitamin and mineral content than chemically treated foods. This gives the food extra flavor! Research indicates that all 21 nutrients show higher levels in organic foods: Magnesium – 28% more; Vitamin C – 26% more; Iron – 20% more; phosphorous – 14% more.

  • 4. RESTORES ECOLOGICAL SYSTEM Due to the soil being organic, there is a reduction in water pollution, soil poisoning, and death of insects or birds. Since the soil is organic, it restores the ecological system, thus, creating a healthy environment for all creatures.

  • 5. BETTER LIFESTYLE Organic gardening can help you lead a better lifestyle! Gardening is labor intensive; therefore, you will release stress, lose weight, and gain extra energy in a short period of time. All these factors can allow you to feel better about yourself.
